DTCSSS Organization

Introduction

Sitio Bebe is a small village in the Philippines where people are facing some serious health problems. Stress is causing mental health issues, and they don’t have clean water, making things even harder. The community is also dealing with hypertension, acute respiratory infections, and acute lower respiratory tract infections. Even though the situation is tough, the people of Sitio Bebe are strong and determined. They need better healthcare and clean water to overcome these challenges and live healthier lives.

Mission

As part of the organization, we believe that every community deserves to live a decent life. As organizers, we want to make a positive change in Sitio Bebe. We're planning to organize a fundraising concert to gather funds and help improve the environment in Sitio Bebe.

Why Fundraising Concert?

We're throwing this concert to support Sitio Bebe! It’s not just a fun event, it’s a way to help out a community that really needs it by jamming to the beat. Plus, we want to share their awesome culture with everyone.

How you can help us execute our mission

When you join our concert, you’re doing more than just having fun and enjoying the beat, you’re also helping Sitio Bebe get the support they need. The money we raise will go towards better healthcare, clean water, and mental health resources for the community.
